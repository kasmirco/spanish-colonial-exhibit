Huellas De Espana en Filipinas

Echoes of the Spanish Era: Through the Colonial Lens

Our Story

Exploring Culture, Fashion, Food and Music: A Time Travel Through the Spanish Colonial Period.
This museum was organized by the students of STI College Global City, from the BSTM-M602, a photo exhibit that presents a visual journey into one of the most influential periods in Philippine history. Headed by Event Head - Aaron Loyola, Assistant Head - Eirine Sixto, and Creative Head - Divine Ginez, the exhibition showcases different photographs personally captured by the students of BSTM-M602.
Featuring images inspired by the Spanish Colonial Period, the exhibit highlights the beauty of Spanish colonial influences through culture, fashion, music, and food that continue to shape Filipino identity today. Each photograph reflects different stories, struggles, faith, and heritage left behind by centuries of Spanish influence.
Presenting photographs that embody what people see, feel, and remember from a time where history still echoes in the present.
